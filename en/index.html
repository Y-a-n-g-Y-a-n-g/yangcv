About

I am a PhD student at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Chonnam National University. I work on dual-arm robot control, image processing, and embodied intelligence, and I welcome collaboration and discussion.

Research

Current topics include:

Dual-arm robot control
Coordination, motion planning, and compliance control for bimanual manipulation.
Image processing and vision
Recognition, tracking, and state estimation for robotic perception.
Embodied intelligence
Integrating perception, planning, and manipulation for embodied agents.
